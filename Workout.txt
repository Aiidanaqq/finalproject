Full-Body Workout (Approximate Time: 60-90 minutes):

Warm-Up (10-15 minutes):

Jogging in place or jumping rope (5-10 minutes)
Muscle and joint stretching (5 minutes)
Strength Exercises (30-40 minutes):

Squats with dumbbells or barbells (3-4 sets of 10-15 repetitions)
Bench press with dumbbells (3-4 sets of 10-15 repetitions)
Chin-ups or pull-ups (3-4 sets of 10-15 repetitions)
Pull-ups on a pull-up bar or with TRX (3-4 sets to failure)
Core Exercises (10-15 minutes):

Plank (3-4 sets of 30-60 seconds)
Side plank (3-4 sets of 30-60 seconds per side)
Abdominal crunches (3-4 sets of 10-15 repetitions)
Cardio (10-20 minutes):

Running on a treadmill or outdoor jogging (10-20 minutes)
Stationary bike or elliptical trainer (10-20 minutes)
Cool Down and Stretching (5-10 minutes):

Stretching all major muscle groups (5-10 minutes)
Breathing exercises and relaxation (5 minutes)
