1.Initial Relaxing Pose (5 minutes):
   Sit on the mat and take several deep breaths, focusing on breathing and relaxing the body.

2.Sun Salutation (10 minutes):
   Repeat the sequence of poses, including Tadasana, Uttanasana, Plank, Chaturanga Dandasana, Upward Facing Dog, and Downward Facing Dog.
  
3.Strength and Flexibility Poses (15 minutes):
   Perform poses like Virasana, Bhujangasana, Balasana, and others to strengthen muscles and improve flexibility.

4.Conclusion and Relaxation (5-10 minutes):
   Transition into Shavasana for final relaxation.
   Breathe slowly and deeply, paying attention to sensations in the body and mind.
